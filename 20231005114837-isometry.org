#+title: Isometry
Let \[X\] and \[Y\] be metric spaces with metrics (distances) \[d_x\] and \[d_y\].
A map \[f: X \to Y\] is called an *isometry* or *distance preserving* if \[\forall a,b \in X, d_x(a,b) = d_y(f(a), f(b))\].
